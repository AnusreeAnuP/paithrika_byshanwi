Who We Are

Paithrika_ByShanwi is a curated ethnic fashion destination built on the love of Indian traditional wear. We bring you hand-picked collections of sarees, kurtis, salwar suits, and set mundus that celebrate the rich textile heritage of India.

Founded with a simple vision — to make premium ethnic wear accessible to every woman — we source our collections directly from skilled weavers and artisans across India.

Every piece in our store has been selected with care, ensuring the finest quality of fabric, craftsmanship, and design.
